## 🧭 Overview

**Hollow Riders** is a dungeon crawler where you play as a **Proxy**, guiding a group of mercenaries through a **Hollow** — a dangerous, ever-shifting space where nothing is quite what it seems.

Plan your path, adapt to the unknown, and lead your team through the chaos.
